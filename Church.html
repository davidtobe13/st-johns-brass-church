About Us

Know more about our Church.

We are a gospel centered church that equips culture and shaping christians

To make disciples through gospel proclamation, baptize those disciples into church of christ and teach them the biblican command of Jesus Christ

We strive to be a place where we are committed to Christ. We believe that relevant worship, fellowship and service are vital for spiritual growth. Therefore we are committed to worshipping GOD passionately, teaching the bible practically and multiplying faith filled disciples through the word of GOD.
